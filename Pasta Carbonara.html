Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • minced garlic cloves
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Boil the Pasta in a pot, add a pinvh of salt.
  2. In a pan saute the garlic.
  3. Beat the eggs and half of the cheese.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in pan.
  5. Add the beaten egg mixture, chesse and salt.
