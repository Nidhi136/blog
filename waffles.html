Waffles

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix together dry ingredients,set aside.Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. In a seperate bowls, beat the eggs.Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla.
  4. pour the mixture in the flour, beat until blended.
  5. Laddle the batter into the preheat waffle iron.
  6. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.
